# A STUDY OF THE WILD- PHOTOGRAPHY WEBSITE
## The most captivating pictures of wildlife

Author- Tracy Mukami Mwingirwa

The study of the wild is a sort of wildlife photography portfolio. It showcases beautiful and captivating photos of wild animals in their natural habitats all while educating on biological facts about them. The author, Tracy, includes her favourite photographs and more information on the animals to educate her readers.
